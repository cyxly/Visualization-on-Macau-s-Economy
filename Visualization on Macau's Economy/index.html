Data Visualization on Macau

-by Group 9

In this project, we are interested in displaying detail of tourism of Macao in a intuitional and interactive way, and try to explore the connection between tourists number and performance of gaming industry.

Visitor

Guess where the most visitors entering Macau come from?! Definitely CHINA!

But which region ranks the second? Which country witness the most decrease and growth in number? And which part of China have more visitors? What’s the most popular season? In this part, we will explore the geographical distribution of the number of visitors in 2008 – 2016.09 and try to explore these questions.

Tourism

Hotel

Tourism is one of the most important part of Macau. The consisting increase in tourists led the fast pace development of the top-rating hotels. However, the celling of the tourist quantity has been reached in recent years while the construction of the new luxury hotels are still in progress. This is a dangerous signal.

Gaming Industry

This part is about gaming industry in Macau, we will study the composition of the gaming and find out the main source of revenue in the gaming. We also explore the relationship between visitors and the revenue of the gaming in this part. And the role of Mainland visitors play in the gaming industry.
